<p>We would also like to remind you of the IEEE policy on reviewing research papers. </br>
<b>-</b> treat the contents of papers under review as privileged information not to be disclosed to others before publication.</br>
<b>-</b>  It is expected that no one with access to a paper under review will make any inappropriate use of the special knowledge which that access provides.</br>
<b>-</b>If you are unsure about the review process or what is expected please follow the <a href="https://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/organizers/paper_review_process.html">Link</a> to review the process. 
</p>

<p>The status reports to reviewers should identify the reviewers of each paper, its present status (accepted/rejected), and any comments regarding the paper's quality or referral to another reviewer. </p>

<p>The deadline for this review is the 12/6/16</p>
